About Me

I am a Data Analyst with over 4 years of experience driving data-driven decisions across healthcare and industrial manufacturing domains. I have hands-on experience building automated ETL workflows, working with cloud platforms such as AWS and Azure, and designing predictive models for forecasting and risk stratification. I’m comfortable partnering with both technical and business stakeholders, building governed analytics solutions, and communicating results in a way that supports strategic decision-making. I hold a Master’s in Data Science from the University of Memphis and enjoy solving real-world problems with clean data, clear stories, and reliable dashboards.
